# Sweet Potato Pie

## Ingredients

* 15 oz sweet potato puree
* 2 tsp speculaas or pumpkin spice mix
* 1/2 tsp salt
* 5 tbsp coconut oil
* 1/3 cup packed light brown sugar
* 1 cup evaporated coconut milk
* 2 medium eggs
* 1 gluten-free grahamn pie crust


## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 425°F.
2. In a large bowl, combine all ingredients except pie crust and eggs. Whisk until smooth.
3. Add eggs and whisk until fully incorporated.
4. Pour mixture into pie crust.
5. Bake for 15 minutes. Reduce oven temperature to 350°F. Bake about 35 minutes until custard reaches 175°F. 
6. Let cool completely on a wire rack.
