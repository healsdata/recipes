# Matt's Favorite Coleslaw

## Ingredients

* 1/3 cup gulden's mustard
* 1 cup mayo
* 2 tbsp lime juice
* 1 tbsp honey
* 1 tsp adobo
* 1lb shredded cabbage
* Black pepper

## Instructions

1. Mix first five ingredients. 
2. Pour over cabbage, mix until coated.
3. Chill overnight. 
4. Add pepper to taste.

## Notes

* First made for Jon's 30th birthday party
